## 1. Abstract
This paper presents the design, optimization, and evaluation of an ultra-lightweight, high-speed mini line follower robot utilizing the Waveshare RP2040-Zero microcontroller. By leveraging a single-cell 3.7V Lithium power architecture coupled with a high-efficiency MT3608 boost topology, the physical payload is minimized. To circumvent the high internal voltage drop constraints of the legacy L293D dual H-bridge driver, a deterministic power-splitting strategy was engineered. The guidance system employs a 5-channel infrared reflectance array analyzed through a Proportional-Derivative (PD) control loop executing at a sub-millisecond refresh cycle, featuring an ultrasonic 20 kHz pulse-width modulation (PWM) layer to eliminate acoustic motor coil resonance.
